Caitlin seeks to expose and share the beauty that lives in this world through photography, storytelling, and art. Having travelled extensively and lived in several different countries, she is most inspired by adventure, chasing the unknown, breath-taking landscapes, the simple things, and a deep nostalgia for the places she once knew and people she has met along the way. Quitting her day job in order to pursue her passions was a bold move, but ultimately necessary to grow, create, and travel.

If you bring her slightly soggy mozzarella sticks she will love you for all eternity, but working together would be fun too.
